Privacy Policy 

Updated: April 1, 2026

BuckIt is built to help you track your time, goals, and personal progress. We are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your data secure.


1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app’s functionality:

 Goals / domains you create  
 Time tracking sessions  
 Notes you add  
 Basic technical data (e.g., app version, device type for stability)

If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive a secure, anonymous user identifier from Apple.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only to:

 Save and display your goals and progress  
 Restore your sessions across app launches  
 Sync your data across your Apple devices  
 Improve app performance and stability  

We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored:

 Locally on your device  
 In your personal iCloud account via Apple CloudKit  

Your data is tied to your Apple ID and remains under your control.

We do not maintain our own external database of your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data may only be processed by Apple services (such as CloudKit) as required to operate the app.

5. Security

We rely on Apple’s secure infrastructure, including:

 Encryption in transit and at rest  
 Secure iCloud storage  
 Device-level protections  

 6. Your Control

You have full control over your data:

 Delete goals or notes anytime in the app  
 Delete the app to remove local data  
 Manage or delete iCloud data through Apple settings  

7. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Continued use of BuckIt means you accept any updates.
