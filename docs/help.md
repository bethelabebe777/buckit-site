# Help

## Getting Started

* Create a goal card to begin tracking your time.
* Set a goal (e.g. study hours or work sessions).

## Using the Timer

* Tap start to begin a session.
* The timer tracks your progress automatically.

## Sessions & Notes

* Add notes during or after a session to track what you worked on.

## Viewing Progress

* Your progress updates as you complete sessions.

## Tips

* Double tap a card to expand it to fullscreen.
* Use consistent sessions to build habits.
